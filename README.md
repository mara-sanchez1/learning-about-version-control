*What you like most about version control?*
I like that it helps to keep things organized and traceable. It makes it easier for other people to understand your workflow and the evolution of it.

*what you find most challenging/confusing/frustrating about version control?*
I think it is challenging when working in group projects, people are changing things simultaneously and it things can get overwhelming very quickly.
